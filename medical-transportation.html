Medical Transportation

Reliable Transportation for Medical Needs

DC Black Cars provides professional non-emergency medical transportation for clients throughout Washington DC, Northern Virginia, and Maryland. We understand that medical appointments require dependable, on-time service — and we take that responsibility seriously.

We serve clients traveling to major medical centers, specialist offices, outpatient procedure facilities, and hospitals throughout the DMV. Many of our medical transportation clients have recurring appointments and rely on DC Black Cars as their consistent ground transportation provider.

Our service is discreet, professional, and focused on making each trip as comfortable as possible. Chauffeurs are patient, punctual, and attentive to the needs of our medical transportation clients.

Recurring Clients: We accommodate clients with regular appointment schedules. Contact us to discuss ongoing transportation arrangements and preferred scheduling.
