How It Works

Your Chauffeur. Your Schedule. Your Terms.

Hourly as-directed service gives you a dedicated vehicle and chauffeur for a set number of hours. There are no per-trip charges, no rebooking between stops, and no uncertainty about whether a car will be available when you need it. Your chauffeur stays with you, adapts to your schedule, and handles every transfer throughout the engagement.

This service is particularly well suited to executives managing a full day of meetings across the DC metro, visiting delegations being hosted across multiple venues, attorneys handling multi-court scheduling, and government officials with security-conscious or unpredictable itineraries.

It is also ideal for VIP visitors arriving from out of town who need a dedicated ground transportation resource for the duration of their stay — airport arrival, hotel, meetings, dinners, and departure.

Minimum Booking: Hourly as-directed service is available with a minimum booking period. Contact us to discuss your specific itinerary and we will advise the appropriate service structure.
