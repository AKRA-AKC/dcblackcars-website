How It Works

Your Chauffeur. Your Schedule.

Hourly as-directed service gives you a dedicated vehicle and chauffeur for a set number of hours. There are no per-trip charges, no rebooking between stops, and no uncertainty about vehicle availability. Your chauffeur stays with you, adapts to your schedule, and handles every transfer throughout the engagement.

This service is well suited to executives managing a full day of meetings across the DC metro, visiting delegations being hosted across multiple venues, attorneys handling multi-court scheduling, and investors running back-to-back meetings during a roadshow.

Hourly service is available with a minimum booking period. Contact us to discuss your specific itinerary and we will structure the appropriate service.
