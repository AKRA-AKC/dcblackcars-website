Transfer Services

Every Connection, Every Terminal

DC Black Cars handles the full range of inter-modal transportation needs — from Amtrak connections at Union Station to cross-airport transfers and long-haul runs to Newark.

Union Station — Washington DC

DC Black Cars provides professional pickup and drop-off service at Washington Union Station for Amtrak arrivals and departures. Whether you are connecting from an Amtrak train to a DC meeting, hotel, or onward flight — or need a car to Union Station for a regional rail departure — we coordinate the transfer around your train schedule.

We also provide Union Station to Richmond transfers, Union Station to Dulles airport runs, and connections between Union Station and all major DC hotels and business districts.

DC to Newark Liberty International (EWR)

DC Black Cars provides executive car service from Washington DC, Northern Virginia, and Maryland to Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR). This is a popular route for clients whose preferred airline, international connection, or pricing makes Newark a better option than DCA or IAD.

We also serve the return route — EWR arrivals traveling to the DC metro area. All vehicle types available including sedans, SUVs, and Sprinters for group EWR transfers.

Airport-to-Airport Transfers

Not all connections happen at the same airport. DC Black Cars handles airport-to-airport ground transfers across the region — from IAD to DCA, from DCA to BWI, and from any DC-area airport to regional airports in Richmond (RIC), Charlottesville (CHO), Baltimore (BWI), or Newark (EWR).

These runs are particularly useful for travelers whose inbound and outbound flights operate from different airports, or for private aviation clients connecting from an FBO to a commercial terminal.

Richmond & Regional Connections

DC Black Cars provides ground transportation between Washington DC and Richmond International Airport (RIC), Richmond Amtrak Station, and destinations throughout central Virginia. We also serve connections from Union Station DC to Richmond and from Dulles or Reagan National to Richmond for clients traveling between the two markets.
