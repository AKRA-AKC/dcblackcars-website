Virginia Wine Country — Without the Designated Driver Problem

Virginia has quietly become one of America's leading wine destinations. With over 300 wineries across the state — including 40+ on the Loudoun Wine Trail alone and the nationally recognized Monticello Wine Trail near Charlottesville — there is no shortage of exceptional tasting experiences within 90 minutes of Washington DC.

DC Black Cars brings the executive Sprinter experience to Virginia wine country. Your private group travels in a luxurious Mercedes Sprinter van with a professional chauffeur who handles all the driving, navigation, and logistics — so your group can focus entirely on enjoying the experience.

This is not a party bus. This is a premium, private wine country experience designed for corporate groups, birthday celebrations, bachelorette parties, and anyone who appreciates the finer things.

Corporate Wine Tours: Looking for a unique team outing or client entertainment experience? A private Virginia wine tour is one of the most memorable corporate events in the DC area. We handle transportation — you handle the toasts.

Loudoun County Wine Trail

Just 30–45 minutes from downtown DC, Loudoun County is home to over 40 wineries set against the backdrop of the Blue Ridge Mountains. This is DC's wine country backyard — and one of the most visited wine regions in the entire country.

Popular stops include Breaux Vineyards, Hillsborough Vineyards, Bluemont Vineyard, Paradise Springs, and The Winery at Bull Run — all within a scenic rolling hills landscape that feels worlds away from the capital.

~2 hrs from DC

Charlottesville & Monticello Wine Trail

Named a nationally recognized wine region, the Charlottesville area and Monticello American Viticultural Area (AVA) is one of Virginia's most celebrated wine regions. With over 40 acclaimed wineries nestled in the hills around Charlottesville, this is a full-day journey worth every mile.

Highlights include Veritas Vineyard, King Family Vineyards, Pippin Hill Farm & Vineyards, Trump Winery, Jefferson Vineyards, and Blenheim Vineyards owned by Dave Matthews. DC Black Cars provides the round-trip executive Sprinter service from DC to Charlottesville and back.

~1.5 hrs from DC

Shenandoah Valley Wine Country

The Shenandoah Valley stretches along the base of the Blue Ridge Mountains and offers some of Virginia's most scenic wine tasting experiences. Wineries like Shenandoah Vineyards, Rappahannock Cellars, and Cave Ridge Vineyard produce wines that rival any in the state.

Our Shenandoah wine tours combine stunning mountain scenery with boutique winery visits for a truly memorable day trip from Washington DC or Northern Virginia.
