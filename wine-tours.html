The Experience

Virginia Wine Country Without the Logistics

Virginia has become one of America's leading wine destinations. With over 300 wineries across the state — including 40+ on the Loudoun Wine Trail and the nationally recognized Monticello Wine Trail near Charlottesville — there is no shortage of exceptional tasting experiences within 90 minutes of Washington DC.

DC Black Cars brings the executive Sprinter experience to Virginia wine country. Your private group travels in a Mercedes Sprinter with a professional chauffeur who handles all driving and logistics — so your group can focus on enjoying the day.

This is a private, premium experience designed for corporate groups, birthday celebrations, bachelorette parties, and anyone who values the quality of the journey as much as the destination.

Wine Regions

Three Premier Wine Regions Within Reach of DC

~45 min from DC

Loudoun County

Just 30–45 minutes from downtown DC, Loudoun County is home to over 40 wineries set against the backdrop of the Blue Ridge Mountains. Popular stops include Breaux Vineyards, Hillsborough Vineyards, Bluemont Vineyard, Paradise Springs, and The Winery at Bull Run. DC Black Cars' most popular wine tour destination.

~2 hrs from DC

Charlottesville

The Charlottesville area and Monticello AVA is one of Virginia's most celebrated wine regions. Highlights include Veritas Vineyard, King Family Vineyards, Pippin Hill Farm, Jefferson Vineyards, and Blenheim Vineyards. DC Black Cars provides round-trip executive Sprinter service from DC to Charlottesville.

~1.5 hrs from DC

Shenandoah Valley

The Shenandoah Valley offers some of Virginia's most scenic wine tasting along the base of the Blue Ridge Mountains. Boutique wineries and mountain scenery make this a standout day trip from Washington DC or Northern Virginia.
