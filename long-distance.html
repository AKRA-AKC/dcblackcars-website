Why Drive Instead of Fly

The Professional Alternative to Regional Air Travel

When you factor in airport check-in time, security lines, boarding, baggage claim, and ground transportation on the other end — a DC to New York flight rarely delivers the time savings it promises. DC Black Cars picks you up at your door and delivers you directly to your destination, without the friction of commercial air travel.

For executives, attorneys, and professionals who value productive travel time, our long distance sedans and Sprinters provide an environment suited to working, resting, or preparing for what lies ahead.

We also serve families and students traveling between Washington DC and universities including Duke, Yale, Penn State, Columbia, and schools throughout the Northeast — particularly during academic breaks and move-in periods.

DC Black Cars regularly handles long-distance runs for attorneys traveling to out-of-town depositions, court appearances, and client meetings, as well as physicians presenting at medical conferences and traveling between facilities across multiple states. If your professional schedule takes you beyond the DC area on a regular basis, we are experienced in supporting that kind of recurring long-distance travel.
