Tour Experiences

Every Way to Experience Washington DC in Luxury

DC Black Cars provides private chauffeured tour experiences for individuals, families, corporate groups, and visiting executives who want to experience Washington DC on their own terms — not on a group bus schedule.

DC Monument & City Tour

A private chauffeured tour of Washington DC's most iconic landmarks. The National Mall, Lincoln Memorial, Jefferson Memorial, Vietnam Veterans Memorial, Martin Luther King Jr. Memorial, the US Capitol, the White House, Georgetown, and Embassy Row — designed around your group's interests and time available.

Ideal for visiting executives, foreign delegations, families, and corporate groups hosting out-of-town guests. Available in sedan, SUV, or Sprinter depending on group size.

DC Nighttime Monument Tour

Washington DC at night is one of the most striking views in any American city. The illuminated Lincoln Memorial, the reflecting pool, the Capitol dome, and the World War II Memorial at night offer a completely different experience than daytime visits. A private nighttime tour in a luxury vehicle is one of the most memorable ways to experience the capital.

Popular with corporate groups, visiting families, and international visitors seeing DC for the first time.

Brewery & Distillery Tours

Washington DC, Northern Virginia, and Maryland have developed a vibrant craft brewery and distillery scene. DC Black Cars provides private group transportation to breweries and distilleries throughout the region — from acclaimed DC breweries like Right Proper and Atlas Brew Works, to Virginia craft distilleries including Catoctin Creek and Copper Fox, to Maryland favorites like Flying Dog in Frederick.

Groups up to 14 in a Mercedes Sprinter. Custom itinerary — you choose the stops. Popular for corporate team outings, bachelor and bachelorette parties, and birthday celebrations.

Cherry Blossom Season Transportation

Washington DC's cherry blossom season in late March and early April draws visitors from around the world to the Tidal Basin and the surrounding National Mall. Parking becomes nearly impossible during peak bloom. DC Black Cars provides private chauffeured transportation during cherry blossom season — drop-off and pickup at the Tidal Basin, Jefferson Memorial, East Potomac Park, and National Mall without the parking and traffic frustration.

Advance booking strongly recommended during cherry blossom season as demand is significant.

Holiday Lights Tours

The Washington DC area transforms during the holiday season with impressive light displays throughout the region. DC Black Cars provides private holiday lights tours for families, corporate groups, and couples — covering downtown DC decorations, the National Zoo's ZooLights, Meadowlark Botanical Gardens, and suburban light displays throughout Northern Virginia and Maryland.

A popular seasonal experience for families and corporate team outings during November and December.

Casino Transportation

DC Black Cars provides professional transportation to and from the region's premier casino destinations — MGM National Harbor in Oxon Hill, Maryland, and Live! Casino & Hotel Maryland in Hanover. Whether you're planning a group outing, a VIP evening, or a regular visit to either property, a professional chauffeur handles the drive so you can focus on the experience.

Sedan, SUV, Sprinter, and limousine options available. Group casino outings welcome. Round trip coordination available.

How It Works

A Private Tour on Your Terms

DC Black Cars' tour experiences are entirely private — your group, your vehicle, your itinerary. There are no other passengers, no fixed departure times, and no rushing through stops because of a group bus schedule. You tell us what you want to see, how long you want to spend at each location, and we build the day around you.

Our chauffeurs know Washington DC and the surrounding region well. They can suggest stops, advise on timing for popular attractions, and adapt the itinerary as your day evolves. This is the most flexible, comfortable, and private way to experience DC — whether you are visiting for the first time or showing the city to guests from out of town.

Tours are booked on an hourly as-directed basis, with a minimum booking period. Contact us to discuss your preferred experience and we will structure the right plan.
