Washington DC's Diplomatic Market

The World's Most Active Diplomatic City Requires a Different Standard of Transportation

Washington DC hosts 182 embassies — more than any other city in the United States. Embassy Row along Massachusetts Avenue, the State Department corridors, the World Bank and IMF headquarters, and the constant movement of foreign officials, bilateral meetings, and diplomatic receptions create a consistent and high-value demand for professional ground transportation that operates with a different standard than conventional executive car service.

DC Black Cars provides ground transportation for embassy staff, visiting foreign delegations, ambassadors, diplomatic mission teams, and the concierge and travel firms that coordinate their logistics. Our chauffeurs understand the protocols of this environment — the discretion expected, the punctuality required, and the confidentiality that is non-negotiable.

We serve the full spectrum of diplomatic transportation needs in Washington DC, from daily staff runs between Embassy Row and government offices, to coordinating full delegation transportation for international summits and bilateral meetings.

Confidentiality is standard on every engagement. Contact us directly to discuss specific requirements, recurring schedules, or delegation logistics for upcoming events.
