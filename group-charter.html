Group Services

Every Type of Group Transportation, Handled Professionally

Conference & Convention Shuttles

Hotel-to-venue shuttle service for conferences and conventions at the Walter E. Washington Convention Center, Gaylord National, and hotels throughout the DMV. We coordinate multi-vehicle schedules to move large groups efficiently.

College Visit Transportation

Group transportation for students and families touring universities in the Mid-Atlantic and Northeast. We have experience with college visits to schools throughout Virginia, Maryland, and long-distance runs to institutions including Duke, Yale, Penn State, Columbia, and others throughout the region.

Wedding Guest Shuttles

Dedicated shuttle service between hotels and wedding venues for your guests. We coordinate pickup schedules, multiple hotel pickup points, and return trips at the end of the evening so no one has to worry about transportation on your wedding day.

Corporate Group Events

Transportation for corporate retreats, team outings, client entertainment days, and multi-stop corporate programs. Sprinters, mini-coaches, and motorcoaches for groups of any size throughout the DC metro area.

Government & Agency Groups

Group transportation for government agencies, delegations, and federal organizations moving between locations throughout Washington DC and the surrounding region.

Sports & Event Groups

Group transportation to Nationals Park, Capital One Arena, Northwest Stadium, and other DC venues. Coordinate your group arrival and departure without the parking challenge.
